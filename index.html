Sudoku Game

How to Play Sudoku

Sudoku is a number puzzle game. The objective is to fill the 9x9 grid so that each row, each column, and each of the nine 3x3 grids contain the digits from 1 to 9 without repeating.

  • Click on an empty cell and enter a number between 1 and 9.
  • Numbers cannot be repeated within a row, column, or 3x3 grid.
  • Use logic and deduction to fill the entire grid correctly.
  • Have fun and challenge yourself!

Tips

Use notes to indicate possible numbers in a cell. Mark the numbers in your temporary notes which are already used in that row, column or the 3x3 grid it belongs to so that you can eliminate some.
